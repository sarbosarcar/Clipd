Why Clipd?

Reliable

Clipd uses base64 encoding and ensures that your clipboard contents are retained in the correct format.

Easy to Use

Clipd is designed to be simple and easy to use. Just paste your clipboard contents and use a link that you can choose!

No Sign Up Required

Clipd does not require you to sign up or create an account. Just paste and share the link.
